If you aren't a fan of Konomi Suzuki, you should become one asap. She's super talented at such a young age, and her voice range is amazing. She debuted in 2013 under Music Factory, and has won the fifth annual Animax Anison Grand Prix. If you're into anisong, you will like Konomin. This is my first Konomin live, and I was super excited when I found that I could attend one of her shows. Joining me at her Tokyo show is @Allocer90 and @matcha_p.

On the way to the Venue

It took 30 minute to get to Zepp Tokyo from Shinjuku. I was at Mikku's Birthday live prior to this, and I had to decide if I wanted to leave Mikku early or arrive late to Konomin. In the end, I decided to miss a bit of Konomin. Hopefully, I made the right choice. When I arrived at Zepp Tokyo, there was a small section for goods and flower stands. Since this live was not a seated event and I arrived late, I was lucky enough to be 10 rows away from the stage. If I had arrived on time, I would be near the back of the venue somewhere. To be honest, the view was amazing and I didn't deserve it.

Closing Remarks

Konomin is awesome, and I think it would have been worth while if I left Mikku earlier to catch this live on time. I wasn't expecting a live band at this event, so that was a plus. Kinda wished I was able to get a concert shirt, but it sold out too fast. I guess this shows how popular Konomin is. I got to hear every song that I wanted expect "DAYS of DASH"... oh well. This live was fun nonetheless and I need to go to another one asap.
