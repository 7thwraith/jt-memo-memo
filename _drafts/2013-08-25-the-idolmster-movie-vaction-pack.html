This week's merchandise (makes it sound like I have something every week even though I don't) involves movie tickets for the Idolm@ster anime film, THE IDOLM@STER MOVIE 輝きの向こう側 (The IDOLM@STER Movie: To the Glittering Other Side) that's going to be shown in Japan some time during the beginning of 2014.

THE IDOLM@STER MOVIE VAC@TION PACK!! is actually the second of limited-edition movie ticket bundles that were sold at THE IDOLM@STER 8th Anniversary Hop! Step!! Festiv@l that was held at Yokohoma not that long ago (8.4.2013).

The first one went on sale at Anime Contents Expo 2013. Unfortunately, I missed my chance to procure one as the every movie pack they had in stock was apparently sold out within Day 01 of ACE2013. Needless to say, this is why I got the second movie pack instead.

The vac@tion pack consists the following:

A clear storage case

You know, it's wouldn't be farfetched to say that I might have gotten this because of that marvelous piece of artwork on the rear side of the case.

Two tickets

Now now, it won't be a ticket bundle if it weren't for tickets.

If anything, the tickets look so pretty that it might actually inhibit anyone from actually using it for admission to the theater.
